Southern Kazakhstan

Southern Kazakhstan is a unique place with hot desert sands, green meadows and mountains, as well as beautiful and picturesque cities and ancient history. By purchasing tours to South Kazakhstan, you will discover the diversity of flora and fauna of this picturesque corner of Kazakhstan. 

Shymkent is the second largest city in Kazakhstan in terms of population, and is also the administrative center of the South Kazakhstan region. The city is included in the state program "Cultural Heritage" as one of the objects for the study and preservation of historical monuments. Now Shymkent is a modern beautiful city. The city has theaters, restaurants, a network of comfortable hotels, where guests and tourists of the city will be received with special oriental hospitality. 

Taraz is one of the oldest cities in Kazakhstan. He is credited with superiority in many ways: the first metropolis, the first mosque built in Kazakhstan, the first madrasah, and so on. Since 2011, excavations have been resumed in the city. This means that archaeologists still have to learn a lot about the mysteries and secrets of the ancient city.
